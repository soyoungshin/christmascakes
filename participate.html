Be an April Christmas Cake with us!

Are you looking for connection? Artists Soyoung Shin and Anthony Bodlović are coming to Seattle and are ready to mingle!
We are looking for individuals who would like to participate in their upcoming experiment in dating, "Christmas Cakes in April." The one-time-only event loosely follows a reality show format where contestants will participate in activities with either Soyoung or Anthony. The winners will receive a complimentary dinner at one of their favorite restaurants in Seattle.
Even if you are simply looking for friendship, want to try something new, or are very much coupled up and need a coffee buddy, we encourage you to apply! All interactions and participations are valid!
The event is for Yellow Fish // Epic Durational Performance Festival XVII, and will be held on April 8, from 1-4 pm, live at the Factory gallery. The event will be recorded in front of a live studio audience.
If you are interested in a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, and the most unique date-story to tell your friends, please email christmascakesinapril@gmail.com with:
1) who you would like to date, Anthony or Soyoung (contestants can only interact with one artist)
2) your name
3) age
4) a picture
5) and a little bit about you.
thanks!
If you're interested, drop us a line! christmascakesinapril@gmail.com
